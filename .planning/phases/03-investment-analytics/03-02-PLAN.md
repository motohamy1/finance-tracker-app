# Plan: 03-02-PLAN - Portfolio UI

**Phase:** 03-investment-analytics
**Goal:** Implement the visual portfolio dashboard with collapsible header, holding cards, and current price management.

---

## 1. Requirements

- **INV-05**: Portfolio overview showing current holdings and unrealized P&L.
- **D-01 to D-04**: Collapsible header, gain/loss background tints, summary stats.
- **D-05 to D-08**: Bulk and inline current price entry with stale price warnings.

---

## 2. Technical Spec

### Portfolio Header
- Reuses the `Animated.View` + `interpolate` pattern from Phase 1's `CategoryHeader`.
- Displays:
  - Total Unrealized P&L (amount + %).
  - Total Realized P&L.
  - Ticker breakdown list.
- Tapping a ticker in the breakdown scrolls to it in the trade list (or filters it).

### Holding Card
- Displays ticker, quantity, average price, current price, and unrealized P&L.
- Background tint: `rgba(34, 197, 94, 0.1)` (green) for profit, `rgba(239, 68, 68, 0.1)` (red) for loss.
- Yellow warning icon if `updated_at` is older than 7 days.

## 4. Risks & Mitigations

| Risk | Impact | Mitigation |
|------|--------|------------|
| Layout shift during collapse | Medium | Use fixed heights for collapsed/expanded states and stable interpolation. |
| Keyboard blocking bulk form | High | Use `KeyboardAvoidingView` or `BottomSheetFlatList` for the price form. |
