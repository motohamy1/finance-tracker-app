# Plan: 03-03-PLAN - History & Filtering

**Phase:** 03-investment-analytics
**Goal:** Enhance trade history with advanced filtering and realized P&L visualization per trade.

---

## 1. Requirements

- **D-09 to D-11**: Three-axis filtering (ticker, direction, date) with bottom sheet and ticker chips.
- **D-12**: P&L badges on individual trade cards.
- **D-13 to D-15**: Grouped per-ticker FIFO pair view.

---

## 2. Technical Spec

### Filtering System
- Ticker Chips: Horizontal scrollable list at the top of the history section.
- Filter Sheet: Bottom sheet containing:
  - Direction toggle (Buy/Sell/All).
  - Date Range Picker (Start/End).
  - Ticker multi-select (or single-select from chips).
- Search Bar: Text input to filter trades by ticker or notes.

### Grouped Pair View
- When a ticker chip is "active" (selected), toggle to a mode that shows `PnLPair` items for that ticker instead of raw `Trade` cards.
- `PnLPairCard`: Compact row showing buy date -> sell date and realized P&L. Expand to see full trade details.

## 4. Risks & Mitigations

| Risk | Impact | Mitigation |
|------|--------|------------|
| Complex state with filters + grouping | Medium | Clearly separate `filteredTrades` from `realizedPairs` in the store logic. |
| Performance with large trade list filtering | Low | Use `useMemo` for derived filtered lists. |
