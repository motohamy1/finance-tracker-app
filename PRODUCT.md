# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Active traders who need fast, unapologetic tools to track investments and expenses on their phones. They are comfortable with bold interfaces, prefer directness over polish, and want to glance at their portfolio and get an immediate read — no soft gradients, no calming pastels. They trade on commutes, during market hours, sometimes at night. The app must feel like a terminal you carry in your pocket.

## Product Purpose

A mobile finance tracker that combines investment P&L tracking (via OCR screenshot import from trading apps) with categorized expense logging. Success means zero friction from screenshot to trade record, and a portfolio view that tells you where you stand in under 2 seconds.

## Brand Personality

- **Unapologetic**: no softening, no "friendly" copy, no hand-holding
- **Raw**: the interface shows its structure — borders, spacing, and hierarchy are the design
- **Bold**: high contrast, high saturation, loud and deliberate

## Anti-references

- SaaS minimalism: no cream backgrounds, no 1px hairlines, no gentle shadows
- Neumorphism: no soft inset shadows, no extruded buttons
- Glassmorphism: no blurs, no frosted panels, no transparency-as-decoration
- "Calm finance": no muted greens, no rounded everything, no pastel cards

## Design Principles

1. **Structure is the decoration** — borders, spacing, and type do the work; no gradients, no decorative imagery
2. **Every pixel earns its place** — if it doesn't communicate state or hierarchy, remove it
3. **Clash on purpose** — color pairings should feel slightly wrong in a good way; safe is boring
4. **Terminal-native** — information density over whitespace; metrics first, labels second
5. **Physical presence** — elements should feel like physical blocks: hard edges, thick borders, real shadows (or none at all)

## Accessibility & Inclusion

- WCAG AA minimum for all text contrast
- Reduced motion support via system settings
- Color-blind safe state indicators (not color alone)
